Our Core Programmes

We offer free technology education to bridge the digital skills gap in under-resourced communities.

After-School Code Clubs

Ages 10-18 | Weekly sessions

Our flagship programme teaches HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to learners in a fun, project-based environment. Students build websites, games, and apps while developing problem-solving skills.

  • 12-week coding curriculum
  • Mentorship from tech professionals
  • End-of-programme showcase event
Enrol a Learner →

Teacher Digital Training

For educators | 6-week course

We equip teachers with the skills to integrate technology into their classrooms. From basic computer literacy to using educational apps and online resources effectively.

  • Certified training programme
  • Classroom technology integration strategies
  • Ongoing support and resources
Register a Teacher →

School Holiday Tech Camps

Ages 12-16 | 5-day intensive

During school breaks, we run immersive technology camps covering robotics, app development, and digital design. A fun and educational way to spend holidays.

  • Hands-on robotics projects
  • Guest speakers from tech industry
  • Certificate of participation
Book a Camp Spot →
