Our Story

Founded in 2018 by five dedicated educators from Soweto, Alexandra, and Tembisa, EduBridge South Africa began as a small weekend coding club in a single community centre.

What started with just 15 students meeting in a church hall has grown into a movement reaching over 2,000 learners annually across 15 partner schools. Our founders witnessed firsthand the technological disparities in their classrooms—students eager to learn but without access to computers or the internet.

Today, EduBridge SA operates two permanent hubs in Soweto and Tembisa, runs after-school programmes in partner schools, and has trained over 120 volunteers to deliver technology education. We believe that digital literacy is not a luxury but a fundamental right for every South African child.

In 2024, we expanded our reach to include teacher training programmes, recognising that empowering educators is just as important as reaching students directly. Our holistic approach ensures that technology education becomes sustainable within communities long after our direct involvement ends.

Our Mission

"To bridge the digital divide by providing accessible technology education and resources to learners in under-resourced South African communities."

We achieve this through free coding programmes, teacher training workshops, and strategic partnerships with schools and corporate sponsors.

Our Vision

"A South Africa where every learner, regardless of their economic background, has equal access to digital literacy and technology skills for the future workforce."

We envision a future where technology education is accessible to all, creating pathways to meaningful employment and economic participation.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at EduBridge SA.

Accessibility

We believe technology education should be free and available to every learner, regardless of their family's financial situation.

Empowerment

We equip learners with skills that open doors to future education and employment opportunities.

Community

We work alongside schools, families, and local organisations to create sustainable change.

Excellence

We deliver high-quality programmes with measurable impact and continuous improvement.
